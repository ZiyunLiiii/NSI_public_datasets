NSI Plastic Phantom CBCT: Paired Metal and Metal-Free Scans

Paired cone-beam CT scans of the same plastic phantom, acquired with and without metal for metal artifact evaluation and correction.

Data:

Two vertical cone-beam CT acquisitions of a plastic phantom collected on an NSI PRD-K416090 system. The paired scans provide matched metal-present and metal-free reference cases.
